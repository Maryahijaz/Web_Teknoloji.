Information About Jordan

The Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan is an Arab country located in southwest Asia,It is located in the middle of the Middle East, located in the southern part of the Levant region and the northern part of the Arabian Peninsula region.
It is bordered by Syria to the north, Palestine to the west, Iraq to the east, and it is bordered to the east and south by the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.
